Professional Window Cleaning Services in Cheshire

Paullouie Window Cleaning – Expert Service, Exceptional Results

At Paullouie Window Cleaning, we combine traditional craftsmanship with modern technology to deliver the finest window cleaning service across Cheshire, Merseyside, and Manchester. With over 10 years of professional experience, we tailor our approach to suit your property's unique needs, using either traditional ladder methods or advanced water-fed pole systems to achieve outstanding results.

Our Window Cleaning Methods

Traditional Ladder Window Cleaning

Our traditional ladder-based approach delivers the most detailed, hands-on finish available. This method allows us to work directly at window level for meticulous cleaning and inspection.

Ideal For:

  • Ground-floor & first-floor windows with safe access
  • Properties with stable, level ground
  • Standard residential homes & bungalows
  • Windows requiring detailed inspection
  • Properties where pole access is restricted

What Makes It Special:

  • Superior Cleaning Results – Hand-cleaned frames, sills & corners
  • Immediate Results – No waiting, instant clarity
  • Personal Attention – Close inspection ensures perfect clarity

Water-Fed Pole System Cleaning

Our advanced water-fed pole technology enables us to clean hard-to-reach windows safely from the ground. Using purified water and specialist brush heads, this method is perfect for upper-storey windows and challenging access points.

Ideal For:

  • Windows above conservatories
  • Upper-storey windows (2nd & 3rd floor)
  • Uneven or soft ground
  • Restricted access areas
  • Large commercial buildings
  • Unsafe ladder conditions

What Makes It Special:

  • Superior Safety – Clean safely from the ground
  • Environmentally Friendly – No chemicals, purified water only
  • Streak-Free Finish – No watermarks or mineral deposits
  • Longer-Lasting Clean – Purified water keeps glass clean longer
  • Frames & Sills Included – Complete window cleaning

Our Comprehensive Service Includes

  • Complete cleaning of all glass panels
  • Frames and sills cleaned thoroughly
  • Removal of cobwebs and debris
  • Spot treatment for stubborn marks
  • Final inspection for perfect clarity
