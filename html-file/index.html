Water-Fed Pole Window Cleaning

Professional Water-Fed Pole Cleaning by Paul Louie Window Cleaning

At Paul Louie Window Cleaning, we use the latest water-fed pole technology to deliver safe, effective, and streak-free cleaning for properties across Cheshire. This advanced system allows us to clean challenging or hard-to-reach areas directly from ground level.

What Is Water-Fed Pole Window Cleaning?

Water-fed pole cleaning is a modern, eco-friendly method that uses purified water delivered through telescopic poles fitted with specialist brush heads. The purified water attracts dirt, lifts it away, and then evaporates naturally to leave a crystal-clear, streak-free finish. No ladders, chemicals, or detergents are needed — just pure water and expert technique.

When We Use the Water-Fed Pole System
  • Windows above conservatories
  • Upper-storey windows
  • Uneven or soft ground
  • Adverse weather conditions
  • Restricted access areas
  • Large commercial properties
Benefits of Water-Fed Pole Cleaning

Superior Safety Standards

Our technicians remain safely on the ground throughout the cleaning process, eliminating ladder risks and protecting both your property and our team.

Environmentally Friendly

No harsh chemicals or detergents — we use only purified water, making this method safe for your family, plants, pets, and the environment.

Flawless Shine

Purified water dries naturally without leaving mineral deposits or streaks, resulting in brilliantly clear windows that stay cleaner for longer.

Frames & Sills Included

Our system cleans not just the glass but also frames, sills, and surrounding areas — removing dirt that traditional methods often miss.

Longer-Lasting Results

Purified water and full-frame cleaning reduce dirt build-up, keeping your windows cleaner for longer compared to conventional cleaning.

No Property Disruption

No ladder marks, no garden disturbance, and no need to access your home — we work efficiently from outside with zero inconvenience.
