About Us

With over 10 years of professional window cleaning experience, we proudly serve customers across Cheshire, delivering reliable, high-quality results every time. We are fully insured, committed to safety, and focused on providing a friendly, dependable service you can trust.

We offer a range of cleaning options to suit your home and schedule, including monthly, bi-monthly, and one-off visits. Our team is equipped to handle both traditional cleaning methods and the water-fed pole system, ensuring the best solution for every type of property.

As part of our commitment to customer satisfaction, we provide:

  • Mobile alerts before each visit
  • Competitive pricing
  • Discounts for regular maintenance customers
  • Windows, frames, and sills cleaned as standard
  • A satisfaction guarantee on every job
  • Refer-a-friend bonuses (existing customers only)

We always prioritise traditional window cleaning, as it delivers the most detailed and streak-free finish. However, when safety becomes a concern — such as cleaning above conservatories, working on uneven ground, or during poor weather conditions — we use the water-fed pole system. This allows us to work safely while still maintaining a high standard of cleaning.

Whether you need a one-off clean or a routine maintenance plan, we are committed to making your windows shine — safely, professionally, and with care.
