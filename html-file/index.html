Why Choose Traditional Ladder Window Cleaning?

Superior Cleaning Results

Traditional window cleaning allows for close inspection and detailed hand-cleaning of every pane, frame, and sill. This hands-on approach ensures we can tackle stubborn marks, remove debris from corners, and deliver a level of perfection that automated methods cannot match.

Immediate Results You Can See

Unlike water-fed pole systems that require drying time, our traditional method provides instantly clear, streak-free windows. You’ll see the difference the moment we finish — no waiting, no watermarks, just crystal-clear glass.

Personal Attention to Detail

Working directly at window level enables us to spot and address issues such as stubborn stains, cobwebs, and accumulated grime. Every window receives individual attention and care.

Water-Fed Pole Window Cleaning

Professional Water-Fed Pole Cleaning by Paullouie Window Cleaning

At Paullouie Window Cleaning, we use the latest water-fed pole technology to deliver safe, effective, and streak-free cleaning for properties across Cheshire. This advanced system allows us to reach heights of up to 60 feet from ground level, making it the ideal solution for challenging or hard-to-reach areas.

What Is Water-Fed Pole Window Cleaning?

Water-fed pole cleaning is a modern, eco-friendly method that uses purified water delivered through telescopic poles fitted with specialist brush heads. The purified water attracts dirt, lifts it away, and then evaporates naturally to leave a crystal-clear, streak-free finish. No ladders, chemicals, or detergents are needed — just pure water and expert technique.

When We Use the Water-Fed Pole System
  • Windows above conservatories
  • Upper-storey windows
  • Uneven or soft ground
  • Adverse weather conditions
  • Restricted access areas
  • Large commercial properties
Benefits of Water-Fed Pole Cleaning

Superior Safety

Our technicians remain safely on the ground throughout, eliminating risks associated with ladder use.

Environmentally Friendly

No chemicals or detergents, only purified water.

Streak-Free Shine

Purified water dries naturally without marks or residue.
