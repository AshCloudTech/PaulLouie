Why Choose Paul Louie Window Cleaning?

Our Professional Window Cleaning Services

At Paul Louie Window Cleaning, we provide both traditional ladder cleaning and modern water-fed pole cleaning to ensure spotless, streak-free windows with the highest level of safety and care.

Traditional Window Cleaning with Ladders

Our traditional ladder-based cleaning provides the most detailed, hands-on finish possible. With over 10 years of experience, this method allows us to access each window up close and ensure a superior shine free from streaks, marks, or residue.

Superior Cleaning Results

Close inspection and professional-grade cleaning for every pane, frame, and sill.

Immediate Results

Instantly clear, streak-free windows — no waiting for drying or evaporation.

Attention to Detail

We clean corners, remove grime, and treat each window with individual care.

Water-Fed Pole Window Cleaning

Using the latest telescopic pole systems and purified water technology, we safely clean high and hard-to-reach windows from the ground. This eco-friendly method is perfect for upper-storeys, conservatories, and difficult access points.

What Is Water-Fed Pole Cleaning?

Purified water is pumped through extendable poles fitted with soft brushes. The water attracts dirt and impurities, lifts them away, and dries naturally to a flawless, streak-free finish — no chemicals or detergents needed.

When Do We Use It?

  • Windows above conservatories
  • Upper-storey windows
  • Uneven or soft ground
  • Adverse weather conditions
  • Restricted access areas
  • Large commercial buildings
